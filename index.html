Research Areas

I am deeply passionate about the intersection of humans and technology. My primary research focus lies in Mixed Reality application designs, a cutting-edge field that offers exciting possibilities for reshaping our interactions with the digital world. I am also keenly interested in the application of Large Language Models in multiple domains, including healthcare, where the goal is to create more intuitive and efficient human-computer interactions.
